About

Eddie Muñoz is an Austin, Texas based character artist currently working with QC Games, a video game development company. He previously worked at Carbine Studios in Aliso Viejo, California helping create various creatures, armors, mounts, and weapons for WildStar; a free-to-play online game. Be sure to follow him on instagram for his latest projects.
